# Innovation Register

Patent and innovation tracking for the Smart Railway Crossing Anomaly Prevention System.

---

## INNOV-0001

**Name:** Risk-Adaptive Multilingual Railway Crossing Safety Monitoring Platform

**Description:**

A railway crossing protection platform designed to combine multi-sensor safety monitoring, AI-assisted risk analysis, false alarm rejection, automated response, and multilingual employee-friendly operation.

**Potential Patent Value:** Medium to High

**Reason:**

The system combines risk-adaptive railway safety logic with practical field usability for multilingual railway operators. The integration of sensor fusion, visual AI confirmation, false alarm rejection, and language-localized command interfaces for Indian railway staff represents a novel operational safety approach.

**Status:** Documented — Phase 0

**Date Registered:** 2025-06-23
